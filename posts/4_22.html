Seems that there is a lot of decision/game theory in planning crowd simulations. Because crowd planning is more interested in the behaviors of people rather than the actual animations, there appears to be a strong psychology component involved with how crowds move. It would be interesting to see more research on how humans think in crowds, and how we can predict their motions based on real-life studies of how these individuals think in crowd movements.
